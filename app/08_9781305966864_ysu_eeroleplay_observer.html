Observer Instructions

Please review the instructions for both of the role play participants. As the observer, you will be evaluating their work based on the rubrics provided.

Assess how each participant performed her or his role, as well as how the class responded to each. Did each participant address the following questions with some specificity?

1. Which evaluation design will be used?

2. Why were other designs not chosen?

3. What information will be provided in this evaluation?

4. How will the data be collected?

5. How will the information be presented?
